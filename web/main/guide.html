Our Models
UBFlix is your go-to platform for discovering movies tailored to your tastes. Powered by a hybrid recommendation system, UBFlix leverages both content-based filtering and collaborative filtering to provide highly relevant suggestions. You can switch between two distinct recommendation modes:
FOR YOU - Personalized Content-Based Recommendations
This mode is designed to cater specifically to your unique movie preferences. By analyzing the genres, directors, actors, and themes of the movies you've watched and rated highly, UBFLIX curates a list of films that align with your taste. If you enjoy sci-fi thrillers or heartfelt dramas, the system ensures that your recommendations stay within your preferred spectrum.
PEERS' PICKS - What Others Like You Enjoy
This mode taps into the power of collaborative filtering, leveraging user data to suggest movies enjoyed by people with similar tastes. Instead of relying solely on content features, UBFLIX analyzes viewing patterns, ratings, and user behavior to bring you films that people like you have loved.
